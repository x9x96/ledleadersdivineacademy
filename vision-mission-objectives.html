Vision , Mission & Objectives

Vision

  Led Leaders Divine Academy is situated at Birnin Kebbi. It is a Crèche, Nursery and Primary school established for children between the ages of 6 months – 11 years for Nursery and Primary.
  It is a world class school equipped with state of the art learning facilities. Led Leaders Divine Academy is a pupil-Centered school. It is a school with Excellence in knowledge and Godliness.
  Our primary focus is to build, nurture and develop tomorrow’s Leaders today morally, academically, and socially, through sound and well articulated and integrated qualitative education. We are bent to offer academic Excellence and Modeling of the Child to be disciplined, responsible and be a citizen of repute who can stand-out among His contemporaries all over the world.
  Decadence has set in right before our eyes with the resultant effect being the falling standing in the educational system which is supposed to be the member of every other sector of the economy.
